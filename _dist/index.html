Yvonne Eadon

Image description: A woman stands in front of a fountain, wearing a blue coat and smiling.

Yvonne M. Eadon (she/her), MLIS, is a doctoral candidate in Information Studies at the University of California, Los Angeles. Her research exists at the intersection of conspiracy theory scholarship, information seeking and behavior, online misinformation/ disinformation, and archival studies. Her most recent publication, "(Not) Part of the System: Resolving Epistemic Disconnect Through Archival Reference," was published in a special issue of Knowledge Organization devoted to Politics, Culture, and the Organization of Knowledge.
